Icon For Hire was founded in 2007 in Decatur Illinois. Ariel Bloomer and Shawn Jump initially just started as a garage band. When they needed a drummer Jump invited a friend named Adam Kronshshagen. The band became official in November 2007. Icon For Hire originally played in small clubs with friends and family in the audience. Then moved onto tours across the United States.
